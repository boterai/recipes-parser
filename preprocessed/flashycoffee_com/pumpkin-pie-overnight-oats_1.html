What Ingredients are in Pumpkin Pie Overnight Oats

Making pumpkin pie overnight oats is surprisingly simple—you probably have most ingredients in your kitchen already. This no-cook breakfast comes together in minutes with pantry staples and creates pure autumn magic.

Ingredients You’ll Need:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup canned pumpkin purée
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy works great)
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

A Few Ingredient Notes:

Don’t stress if you’re out of one specific ingredient—overnight oats are wonderfully forgiving. You can swap milk types freely, use vanilla yogurt if plain isn’t available, or adjust sweetness to your taste. The beauty of this recipe is that it practically begs for personalization!

How to Make these Pumpkin Pie Overnight Oats

Save for later!

To create your delicious pumpkin pie overnight oats, combine 1/2 cup rolled oats, 1/2 cup canned pumpkin purée, 1/2 cup milk, 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt, 1 tablespoon maple syrup, and 1/2 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice in a container or mason jar—honestly, this is where the magic happens, and you’ll love how quickly it comes together.

Stir everything until smooth and creamy, making sure those oats are fully coated with the pumpkin mixture (no dry bits left behind!). Once combined, cover your container and refrigerate overnight, or for at least a few hours if you’re short on time.

The next morning, give it a good stir and adjust the consistency by adding more milk if it’s thicker than you prefer—you’re aiming for that perfect spoonable texture.

Pour your overnight oats into a bowl, top with whipped cream or a sprinkle of cinnamon if you’re feeling fancy, and dig in immediately.

If you want to level up the nutrition game, a scoop of protein powder mixed in beforehand works wonders, turning your breakfast into a powerhouse that’ll keep you satisfied until lunch.

Step 1. Combine Pumpkin and Milk

Pour your milk into a bowl or jar, then stir in the canned pumpkin purée until well combined.

Make sure there are no lumps—you want a smooth, creamy mixture that’ll make your oats absolutely delicious!

Step 2. Add Greek Yogurt and Spices

Stir in your Greek yogurt until fully incorporated, then sprinkle in the pumpkin pie spice.

Mix thoroughly so the warm spices distribute evenly throughout your creamy pumpkin mixture—this is where the magic happens!

Step 3. Stir in Maple Syrup

Drizzle in your maple syrup and stir vigorously until it’s completely blended throughout the mixture.

This natural sweetness transforms your oats into a delicious fall treat, so don’t skip this essential step!

Step 4. Blend Until Smooth Consistency

Pour your mixture into a blender and blend on high speed for 30-45 seconds until you reach a smooth, creamy consistency.

If it’s too thick, add a splash of milk and blend again.

You’ve got this!

Pro Tips

To make your pumpkin pie overnight oats even better, you’ll want to know some helpful tricks that’ll take your breakfast from good to amazing!

These pro tips will help you create the perfect bowl every single time:

  • Prep containers in advance – Mix your oats and ingredients in individual jars so you’re ready to grab and go during busy mornings.
  • Adjust thickness to your preference – Add more milk for a creamier texture or less milk if you prefer thicker oats.
  • Layer your toppings strategically – Place granola and nuts on top right before eating to keep them crunchy and delicious.
  • Experiment with different milk options – Try almond, oat, or coconut milk to discover your favorite flavor combination.
  • Store up to five days – Make multiple jars at once for convenient grab-and-go breakfasts throughout your week.
  • Consider premium matcha for a unique twist that can enhance the flavor profile and health benefits of your oats with high end matcha. Adding a premium matcha blender to your kitchen tools can elevate the blending process for a smoother consistency and richer taste.

Pumpkin Pie Overnight Oats Variations and substitutions

Now that you’ve mastered the basic pumpkin pie overnight oats recipe and learned some awesome pro tips, it’s time to get creative with variations and substitutions that’ll keep your breakfast exciting all season long!

You don’t have to stick with the original recipe.

Try these fun swaps:

  • Milk options: Use almond, oat, or coconut milk instead of dairy milk.
  • Protein boost: Add Greek yogurt or protein powder for extra nutrition.
  • Sweetener swap: Replace maple syrup with honey or brown sugar. This can be an excellent choice since honey has a lower glycemic impact due to its natural sweetness.
  • Texture change: Mix in granola, nuts, or chocolate chips for crunch.
  • Spice variations: Add nutmeg, ginger, or vanilla extract for different flavors.

These simple changes let you customize your overnight oats to match your taste preferences. Plus, you could even enjoy them with a hot cup of specialty coffee beans on the side! You’ll discover new favorite combinations while keeping the delicious pumpkin pie flavor you love!
