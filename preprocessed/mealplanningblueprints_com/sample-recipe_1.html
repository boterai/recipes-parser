Easy Chicken Soup

This easy chicken soup is packed with vegetables and tender chicken. Perfect for a cozy dinner.

Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 30 minutes Total: 45 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 carrots, sliced
  • 2 celery stalks, sliced
  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup frozen peas

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add carrots and celery, cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Add chicken, broth, salt and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes until chicken is cooked.
  4. Remove chicken, shred it, and return to pot. Add peas and cook for 5 more minutes.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!

Notes

You can substitute the chicken breast with chicken thighs for a richer flavor. This soup freezes well for up to 3 months.
