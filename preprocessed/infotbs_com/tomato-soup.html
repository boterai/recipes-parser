Simple Tomato Soup

Quick and easy tomato soup made with fresh ingredients. Perfect for a cozy lunch or dinner!

Prep: 10 minutes

Cook: 25 minutes

Total: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800 g canned tomatoes
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream (optional)
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 minute more.
  4. Pour in canned tomatoes and vegetable broth. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.
  6. Use an immersion blender to puree the soup until smooth.
  7. Stir in sugar, salt, and pepper. Add cream if desired.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil.

Cook's Note

You can substitute fresh tomatoes for canned when in season. This soup freezes well for up to 3 months.
