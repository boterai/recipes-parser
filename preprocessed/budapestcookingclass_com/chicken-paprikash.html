Chicken Paprikash

Classic Hungarian chicken paprikash with sour cream sauce. A comfort food favorite from Budapest.

Prep: 15 minutes Cook: 45 minutes Total: 1 hour

Servings: 4

Category: Main Dish

Cuisine: Hungarian

Nutritional Info

420 kcal; 35/24/15

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg chicken thighs
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons sweet paprika
  • 1 teaspoon hot paprika
  • 200 ml sour cream
  • 1 tablespoon flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 250 ml chicken broth
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • Salt and black pepper

Directions

  1. Season chicken thighs with salt and pepper.
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over medium heat. Brown chicken on both sides, about 8 minutes. Remove and set aside.
  3. Add onions to the skillet and sauté until golden, about 6 minutes.
  4. Stir in both paprikas and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Add bell pepper and tomatoes, cook for 3 minutes.
  6. Return chicken to pan, pour in broth. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes until chicken is cooked through.
  7. Mix sour cream with flour until smooth. Stir into the sauce and heat through without boiling.
  8. Serve with Hungarian noodles or dumplings.

Tips

Make sure not to let the sauce boil after adding sour cream, as it may curdle. The combination of sweet and hot paprika gives this dish its authentic flavor.
