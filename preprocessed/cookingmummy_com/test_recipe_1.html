Chocolate Cake

A delicious chocolate cake recipe that's moist and rich

Prep: 20 minutes Cook: 45 minutes Total: 1 hour 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 3/4 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup strong black coffee
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C). Grease and flour two 9-inch round baking pans.
  2. In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Add eggs, buttermilk, coffee, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
  4. Pour batter into prepared pans and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Cool in pans for 10 minutes, then remove to wire racks to cool completely.

Notes

For best results, use room temperature ingredients and don't overmix the batter.
