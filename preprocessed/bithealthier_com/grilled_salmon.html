Grilled Salmon with Vegetables

Perfectly grilled salmon fillet served with roasted seasonal vegetables and a lemon butter sauce.

Prep: 15 min Cook: 25 min Total: 40 min

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces salmon fillets
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 whole lemon
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 whole zucchini
  • 2 whole bell peppers
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat (around 400°F or 200°C).
  2. Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels and brush both sides with olive oil.
  3. Season the salmon with salt and pepper on both sides.
  4. Cut the zucchini and bell peppers into chunks, toss with remaining olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Place salmon skin-side down on the grill and cook for 4-5 minutes without moving.
  6. Flip the salmon carefully and cook for another 3-4 minutes until it reaches your desired doneness.
  7. While salmon cooks, grill the vegetables for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally.
  8. In a small pan, melt butter with minced garlic and lemon juice.
  9. Serve salmon with grilled vegetables, drizzle with lemon butter sauce, and garnish with fresh dill.

Chef's Notes

For best results, use fresh wild-caught salmon. The internal temperature should reach 145°F (63°C) for medium doneness. You can also bake this recipe in the oven at 400°F for 12-15 minutes if you don't have a grill.
