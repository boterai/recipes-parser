Healthy Chicken Salad

A delicious and healthy chicken salad with fresh vegetables and a light dressing. Perfect for lunch or dinner.

Prep Time: 15 minutes Cook Time: 20 minutes Total Time: 35 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked chicken breast
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 cups mixed greens

Instructions

  1. Dice the cooked chicken breast into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Halve the cherry tomatoes and slice the cucumber.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the chicken, tomatoes, cucumber, and mixed greens.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  5. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss well to combine.

Notes

You can substitute the chicken with grilled tofu for a vegetarian version. Feel free to add other vegetables like bell peppers or avocado.
