Durian Chia Seed Pudding

by Edward Daniel Friday 2 November 2018
written by Edward Daniel Friday 2 November 2018
3K

Durian Chia Seed Pudding; with custard-like velvety durian and silky chia seeds kissed with creamy rich almonds.

Touch forbidden fruit, reimagined with elegance. And, create an exotic satisfying experience.

This dish will overpower any myths that vegan food can’t be utterly divine and nourishing.

When to make:

Make this:

  • When you’re craving a luxurious dessert. Serve this after a rich meal for an exotic finish that’s both unique and indulgent
  • When you’re yearning for a midnight snack — satisfy your cravings with this silky, creamy pudding that’s as comforting as it is intriguing
  • When you’re after a breakfast surprise — wake up to something bold and different — a nourishing, exotic start to your day that’s packed with flavour and nutrients
What inspired me:

Durian Chia Seed Pudding came about because I had wanted to come up with a breakfast treat. Something nourishing that would set me up for the day while writing on my next book. This dish is simple to put together. All it needs is time to be left. This dish is for one person.

Recipe Highlights:
  • Vegan and paleo-friendly
  • A silky, custard-like texture with a nutty crunch
  • Packed with nutrients and healthy fats
  • A bold, exotic twist on a classic pudding
What equipment to use:

For this dish, I suggest:

High-speed blender

Durian Chia Seed Pudding

#
Serves: 1 Prep Time: 15 mins
Nutrition facts: 200 calories 20 grams fat
Rating: 5.0/5
( 1 voted ) 0

Ingredients

  • 100g durian flesh (or 250g with stone)
  • 25g chia seeds
  • 250ml vegan milk

Instructions

  1. Place the durian flesh and vegan milk in a blender and combine well. Transfer to a serving bowl or a tall glass.
  2. Stir in the chia seeds and set aside for a couple of hours or overnight.
