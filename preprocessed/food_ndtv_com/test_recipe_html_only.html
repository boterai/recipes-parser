Butter Chicken

Classic butter chicken recipe with tender chicken in a rich and creamy tomato sauce. This popular North Indian dish is perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 500 grams boneless chicken
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste

How to Make Butter Chicken

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt and spices for 2 hours.
  2. Grill the chicken until cooked through.
  3. Heat butter in a pan and add tomato puree.
  4. Add cream and garam masala.
  5. Add grilled chicken and simmer for 10 minutes.

Cooking Tips

Use Kashmiri red chili powder for the authentic color without too much heat.
