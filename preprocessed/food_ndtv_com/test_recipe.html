Pav Bhaji Recipe

Pav Bhaji is a popular Mumbai street food dish that combines spiced mashed vegetables (bhaji) with soft bread rolls (pav). This flavorful and hearty dish is perfect for lunch or dinner.

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 30 minutes
  • Total Time: 45 minutes
  • Category: Main Course
  • Cuisine: Indian

Ingredients

  • 4 medium potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 cup cauliflower florets, boiled
  • 1/2 cup green peas, boiled
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 capsicum, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons pav bhaji masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • Salt to taste
  • 8 pav buns
  • Fresh coriander leaves, for garnish
  • Lemon wedges, for serving

Method

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add the tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and mushy.
  4. Add the capsicum and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the boiled potatoes, cauliflower, and peas. Mash the vegetables well.
  6. Add pav bhaji masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder, and salt. Mix well.
  7. Add water to adjust consistency and simmer for 10 minutes.
  8. Add the remaining butter and mix well.
  9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with pav buns and lemon wedges.

Chef's Note

For an authentic taste, use plenty of butter. You can also add a dollop of butter on top before serving. Adjust the spice level according to your preference.
